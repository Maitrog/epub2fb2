What is preserved

The converter carries over metadata, the cover, chapter order from the spine, NCX or EPUB 3 navigation, footnote links, text formatting, and binary images.

Limitations

EPUB ZIP entries must be unencrypted. Compressed EPUB files use the browser DecompressionStream API; older browsers may require Chrome, Edge, or a current Firefox version.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the file uploaded to a server?

No. EPUB parsing and FB2 generation run in your browser on your device.

How does Image notes mode work?

Each image is replaced with a footnote link, and the image itself is placed in a separate notes section.

Why does the table of contents remain usable?

FB2 readers build the table of contents from nested section/title nodes. The app maps the EPUB TOC to the spine and creates that structure.
